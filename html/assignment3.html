About College

Newtons Institute of Engineering College of Engineering (NIE) was established in the year 2001 by Lincolns Educational Society, Hyderabad. It is a UGC Autonomous college, approved by AICTE, and is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Kakinada. We offer undergraduate B.Tech programmes in CSE, DS, AI, AI&ML, ECE, EEE, Civil and Mechanical; postgraduate M.Tech programmes with specialisation in CSE with AIML, Computer Science & Engineering, Embedded Systems & VLSI Design, Communication Systems, Electrical Machine Drives, Power Systems, Thermal Engineering, CAD/CAM, Structural Engineering, Transportation Engineering; MBA & MCA ; Diploma Courses:, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML), Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE), Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE), Civil Engineering (CE), Mechanical Engineering (ME) The college is accredited by NAAC with B++ grade Newton's Institute of Engineering (NIE) is one of the most sought-after institutes for aspiring engineers to turn their dreams into reality. Our core strength lies in delivering quality education that meets global standards, while also fostering innovation and research. NIE is committed to addressing societal needs by equipping students with the technical expertise, ethical grounding, and practical skills required to excel in a rapidly evolving world.
